The Platform

One module. Complete fleet intelligence.

The AxisFleet module installs on any truck — whether it uses load cells or air suspension. It captures weight, location, speed and driver behaviour simultaneously and transmits everything live to your dashboard.

Live Axle Weight Monitoring
Per-axle weight captured in real time with ±1% accuracy. Instant overload and underload flags before the truck moves.
GPS Tracking & Zone Detection
Live location and route tracking. Automatic detection of time at mine, on road and at destination — full cycle analysis.
Driver Identification & Scoring
Fingerprint login on the onboard console. Every event linked to a named driver. Monthly driver performance scores automatically calculated.
Hard Braking & Safety Events
Accelerometer flags harsh braking and records the last 10 seconds of movement before any impact or rollover incident.
Automated Fleet Reports
Daily summaries and monthly fleet intelligence reports delivered automatically. Perfect loads, overloads, underloads — all counted and ranked.
Configurable Thresholds
All weight limits, speed thresholds and braking flags are set on a separate configuration page — fully editable by your fleet manager.
Who It's For

Built for any fleet that moves heavy loads.

AxisFleet works on trucks with load cells already installed, and on trucks with air suspension systems. If your truck can be weighed, AxisFleet can capture it.

Mining & ROM Transport
ROM coal, ore, aggregate and bulk mineral fleets. High tonnage, mine permit operations, PBS combinations and abnormal load transport.
Heavy Haul & Long Distance
Interlink and superlink combinations operating on public roads. Compliance with National Road Traffic Act GCM limits on every trip.
Agricultural & Bulk Commodity
Sugar cane, grain, silage, timber and other agricultural bulk transport where payload optimisation directly impacts farm-to-mill profitability.
