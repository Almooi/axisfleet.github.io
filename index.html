The Hardware

Fits any truck.
No rip-and-replace.

The AxisFleet Load Module sits on any axle group. It works with your existing load cells or air suspension -- no new infrastructure, no downtime. Wireless between modules, GSM to the platform the moment a load is registered.

Protection
IP67
Mine-rated enclosure. Dust-tight and waterproof.
Connectivity
Wireless
No cabling between axle modules. GSM to the platform in real time.
Compatibility
Any Truck
Works with existing load cells or air suspension. Single-tipper to road train.
Scale
18 Modules
