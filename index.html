The Problem

Your fleet generates data. Most of it goes nowhere.

Transport companies collect enormous amounts of data from their trucks every day — GPS, axle weight, speed, driver behaviour. But very few extract its full value. That gap is costing you money, safety, and efficiency.

01
Overloaded trucks leave undetected
Trucks depart overloaded with no warning — until they hit a weigh bridge or worse, cause an accident.
02
No real-time visibility
Fleet managers sit in the office with zero live view of what is happening on the road right now.
03
Dangerous driving goes unpunished
Hard braking, speeding and aggressive driving happen daily with no accountability or record.
04
Manual reporting wastes time
Someone manually compiles reports from incomplete information. The data is always stale, always incomplete.
The Solution

One module. Complete fleet intelligence.

The AxisFleet module installs in each truck and captures axle weight, driver behaviour and GPS data simultaneously — transmitting it in real time to your dashboard.

Live Overload Alerts
Flagged at the source before the truck moves. Every axle, every load, every time.
Driver Accountability
Hard braking, speeding and risky behaviour logged per driver — not just per truck.
Automated Reports
Daily, weekly and monthly reports delivered automatically. Perfect loads vs overloads vs underloads.
Real-Time Tracking
Live map view of every truck. Route efficiency, turnaround times and dwell time analysis.
Driver Identification
Login system ties every trip, every event and every incident to a specific driver — not just a vehicle.
Operational Insights
Identify inefficiencies, reduce unnecessary trips and extend vehicle lifespan before problems develop.
How It Works

From raw data to real decisions — in minutes.

01
Module Installed

02
Data Captured
Axle weight, GPS, speed and driver behaviour captured simultaneously in real time.
03
Transmitted Wirelessly
Data transmitted via SIM card to the AxisFleet cloud platform within minutes of any event.
04
You Decide
Live dashboard alerts, automated reports or raw data feed — your choice, your format.
